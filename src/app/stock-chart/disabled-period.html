This sample visualizes stock chart with area.

This sample renders the stock chart without period selector, data's can be navigated through range selector.

In this example, you can see how to render and configure the Stock chart and disable the period selector LineSeries is used to represent selected data value.


Injecting Module:

Stock Chart features are segregated into individual feature-wise modules. To use Candle Series, DateTime axis in stock chart, we need to inject CandleSeriesService and DateTimeService into the @NgModule.providers section.
