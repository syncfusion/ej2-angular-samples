This sample visualizes stock chart with OHLC series.

In this example, you can see how to render and configure the Stock chart. HILO Open Close series is used to represent selected data value.


Injecting Module:

Stock Chart features are segregated into individual feature-wise modules. To use OHLC Series and DateTime axis in stock chart, we need to inject HiloOpenCloseSeriesService and DateTimeService into the @NgModule.providers section.
