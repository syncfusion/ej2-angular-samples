This sample illustrates a chart with candle series and an accumulation distribution indicator. The trackball shows information about the stock rates and signal values each day.

In this example, you can see how to render and configure an accumulation distribution indicator. This uses volume and price to identify whether stock is accumulated or distributed. It also identifies divergences between the stock price and volume flow.

Injecting Module

Chart component features are segregated into individual feature-wise modules. To use Accumulation Distribution Indicator, we need to inject AccumulationDistributionIndicatorService into the @NgModule.providers section.

Tooltip tooltip is enabled in this example. To see the tooltip in action, hover over a point or tap on a point in touch enabled devices.More information on the Accumulation Distribution Indicator can be found in this documentation section.
